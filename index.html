Our Story

Where Light
Becomes Art

I grew up watching how a single well-placed lamp could change the entire mood of a room. Light, I discovered early, is the most underestimated element of any interior — it shapes how we feel in a space far more than furniture or paint ever could.


" I didn't start Lumina to sell lights. I started it to help people fall in love with their own spaces — one carefully chosen light at a time.
— Shikhar Gupta, Founder

Lumina was born right here in Lakhimpur Kheri — a city I'm proud to call home. My vision was simple: to bring the kind of world-class designer lighting you'd find in a Mumbai or Delhi luxury showroom, directly to the people of our region. Every piece in our collection is handpicked by me personally, chosen not just for its beauty, but for the story it tells and the feeling it creates.


Whether you're furnishing a new home, renovating a cherished space, or dreaming of something entirely unique — I'm here to guide you. At Lumina, you don't just walk out with a light fitting; you walk out with a piece of design that will define your space for years to come.

Contact Us
What brands and styles of lights do you carry?
We carry a wide range of designer lighting styles — from classical crystal chandeliers and Art Deco pieces to modern geometric fixtures and contemporary LED designs. Our collection is sourced from premium manufacturers across India and spans all major categories: chandeliers, pendants, wall sconces, table lamps, floor lamps, ceiling lights, and outdoor fixtures.
Do you deliver outside of Lakhimpur Kheri?
Yes, we deliver across Uttar Pradesh and can arrange shipping to other states across India as well. Delivery timelines and charges vary by location. Please contact us for a delivery quote to your area.
What materials are your lights made from?
Our lights are crafted from premium materials including crystal, brass, stainless steel, iron, aluminium, fabric, and hand-blown glass. Each product listing specifies the materials used. We prioritise durable, high-quality materials that ensure both beauty and longevity.
Do you provide installation services?
We can recommend trusted electricians and installation professionals in Lakhimpur Kheri and nearby areas. For out-of-city orders, we provide detailed installation guides with each product. All our fixtures are designed for straightforward installation by a qualified electrician.
What is your return and exchange policy?
We accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery for products that are unused and in their original packaging. Please contact us immediately if you receive a damaged or defective product and we'll resolve it promptly.
Can I visit your showroom to see the lights in person?
Absolutely! We warmly welcome you to our showroom at Ram Nagar Colony, Near Green Field Academy, Lakhimpur Kheri, UP. Seeing the lights in person — glowing and installed — is truly the best way to appreciate their beauty. No appointment needed, just walk in during business hours.
Do you cater to bulk or commercial orders?
Absolutely! We regularly supply lighting to hotels, restaurants, offices, banquet halls, and residential projects. For bulk orders, we offer special pricing and dedicated assistance from Shikhar to help you choose the right fixtures for each space. Please contact us directly with your project details for a quote.
